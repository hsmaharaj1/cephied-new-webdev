Our Goal

Here at Cepheid we try to bring awareness in the field of Astronomy and Astrophysics.

Welcome to CEPHEID, the twinkling heart of IIT Hyderabad's night sky! Our astronomy club offers a celestial playground where students dive into the vast expanse of the cosmos. From mesmerizing stargazing sessions to thrilling discussions on black holes and beyond, CEPHEID ignites a passion for exploration. We organize captivating events, from lunar eclipses to meteor showers, providing an immersive journey into the mysteries of the universe. Join us under the star-studded canvas as we unravel the secrets of the cosmos, fostering curiosity and wonder one constellation at a time.
